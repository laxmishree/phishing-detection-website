CYBER SECURITY EDUCATION

Learn how to identify and protect yourself from phishing attacks

⚠ What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where criminals impersonate trusted entities to steal sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, and personal data.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Always verify the sender's email address carefully
  • Look for spelling errors and unusual formatting
  • Never click links in unexpected emails

✉ Email Scam Tactics

Scammers craft convincing emails that create urgency and trick users into clicking malicious links.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Avoid urgent requests
  • Report suspicious emails

💳 Banking Fraud Awareness

Fake banking websites attempt to steal financial credentials.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Type bank URLs directly
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Monitor accounts regularly

🔗 Social Media Scams

Social media platforms are common targets for phishing attacks.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Be cautious of unknown accounts
  • Avoid shortened URLs
  • Verify giveaways and promotions

🌐 Fake Website Detection

Fake websites mimic legitimate sites to steal your information.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Check HTTPS certificates
  • Look for domain misspellings
  • Use PhishGuard AI to verify websites

📱 Mobile Phishing (Smishing)

SMS phishing uses text messages containing malicious links.

🔒 PROTECTION TIPS

  • Never click unexpected SMS links
  • Verify the sender
  • Install security software
